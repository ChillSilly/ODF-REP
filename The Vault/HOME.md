# The Abyss — Complete Trading Vault

> *The market is a dark place. The deeper you go, the clearer it becomes.*
> *Edge emerges not from seeing flow, but from identifying when structural constraints force traders to behave in a non-optimal way.*

---

## What This Vault Is

A complete, integrated knowledge system for trading futures using:
- **Macro narrative** (regime identification, central banks, catalyst timing)
- **Options flow** (GEX, dealer hedging, OPEX, sweeps)
- **Order flow** (footprint, tape, DOM, heatmap, delta, VWAP)
- **Market structure** (AMT, volume profile, TPO, market profile)
- **Risk management** (Bayesian Kelly, convexity, EV, drawdown)

**Primary goal:** Top and bottom ticking futures with institutional-grade confluence.
