## Definition

The Volume Point of Control (VPOC) is the price level with the absolute highest traded volume in a given time period. It represents maximum acceptance and two-sided agreement.

**Properties:**
- Acts as a magnet for price — tendency to revert to VPOC
- Often becomes support in uptrends, resistance in downtrends
- More significant than Market Profile POC (time-based) for futures trading

---

## Using VPOC

**Current session VPOC:** Acts as a magnet during the session. Target from extremes.

**Naked VPOC (untested from prior session):** One of the strongest future support/resistance levels. Unfinished auction business.

**Composite VPOC (monthly/quarterly):** Major institutional reference level. Major tops and bottoms often form far from the composite VPOC — the further from long-term value, the more vulnerable the extreme.
