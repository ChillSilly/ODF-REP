# Short Gamma Regime

**Tags:** `#regimes`
**Links:** [[Concepts/GEX]] · [[Bridges/Bridge - Regimes to Strategies]] · [[HOME]]

**Characteristics:** GEX negative, dealers amplify moves, trending/volatile market.
**Strategy:** Trend-follow breakouts, avoid fading strong moves, expand stops.
**Duration:** Often 1–2 weeks following a major vol event or OPEX.
