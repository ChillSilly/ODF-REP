# Long Gamma Regime

**Tags:** `#regimes`
**Links:** [[Concepts/GEX]] · [[Bridges/Bridge - Regimes to Strategies]] · [[HOME]]

**Characteristics:** GEX positive, dealers dampen moves, mean-reverting market.
**Strategy:** Fade extremes to VWAP/POC, range trade between call/put walls, tight stops.
**Duration:** Typically pre-OPEX week and early in a new monthly cycle.
